   - **LLM Used:** LLaMA2-7b (English), Baichuan2-7b (Chinese)

   - **LLM Used:** Transformer-based encoder/decoder; BERT for distillation

   - **LLM Used:** BERT (encoder), LSTM (decoder)

   - **LLM Used:** GPT-2

   - **LLM Used:** CTRL (generation), BERT (semantic classifier)
13. Natural language watermarking via paraphraser-based lexical substitution \cite{qiang2023natural}

14. Rewriting-Stego: generating natural and controllable steganographic text with pre-trained language model \cite{li2023rewriting}
   - **LLM Used:** BART (bart-base2)

   - **LLM Used:** BERT (Google’s BERTBase, Uncased)
